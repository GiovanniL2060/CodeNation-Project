What are Enviornemntal Refugees?

Environmental refugees are immigrants who are forced to flee because of the changes in the climate and natural disasters, such as volcanoes and tsunamis.Also, there are many large groups of immigrants that classify as environmental refugees such as Climate Refugees. Due to their unpleasant conditions these refugees had to face they were forced to go to unfamiliar places with no knowledge of how to survive.
